Accessibility
Transit
Conveniently, there are public transit stations on the same block as the Armory. By train, take the Red Line to Thorndale. The nearest accessible Red Line stations are Granville (½ mile north) and Bryn Mawr (½ mile south). By bus, take the 36 to Broadway & Thorndale.
